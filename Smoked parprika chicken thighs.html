ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon granulated garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon ancho chile powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground tumeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon cider vinegar
  • 4 large bone-in,skin on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley (optional)

how to make the recipe!

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).Greaes an 8x8 inch baking dish
  2. In a large resealable stroage bag,combine paprika,garlic,ancho chile poweder,tumeric, and black pepper;add olive oil and apple cidder vinegar and mix together.Place chicken thighs into the bag,reseal, and massage seasoning mix into chicken. Marinate,refrigerated,for at least 15 minutes.
  3. Place the chicken in the preparede baking dish and drizzle with melted butter
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the chicken is no longer pink inside and the skin is crispy, about 40 minutes, brushing occasionaly with the pan drippings.An instant-read thermometer inserted into the thick part of a thigh should read at least 165 Degrees F(74 degrees C).For extra crsipy skin, place under the broiler for 1 to 2 minutes.Garnish with Parsley
